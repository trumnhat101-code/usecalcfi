How to Lower Your $30,000 Car Payment

  • Put more down: A larger down payment or trade-in reduces the amount financed and your monthly payment.
  • Improve your credit: Buyers with 720+ scores get the lowest APRs; even a small rate drop saves real money over the term.
  • Choose a shorter term: 48 or 60 months costs less interest than 72–84 months, though the monthly payment is higher.
  • Shop multiple lenders: Banks, credit unions and online lenders price the same borrower differently — get a few quotes before financing at the dealer.
