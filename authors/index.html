Editorial accountability

Nathan Le currently researches, writes, and maintains UseCalcFi's calculators and educational guides. Each calculator is tested with worked examples and checked against primary sources such as IRS publications, Social Security Administration tables, Federal Reserve releases, and CFPB guidance. Pages that depend on annual limits or tax rules are reviewed when new official figures are released.

UseCalcFi identifies the author on its guides, shows review or update dates where the information is time-sensitive, and explains formulas and assumptions on calculator pages. Our editorial standards and methodology describe how sources are selected and how commercial relationships are kept separate from calculations.

Readers can review Nathan's full author profile and areas of focus, explore UseCalcFi's original data studies, or report a correction. UseCalcFi provides educational estimates, not personalized financial, tax, or legal advice.
