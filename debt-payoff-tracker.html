Snowball vs Avalanche — which is better?

Snowball pays the smallest balance first — you knock out debts fast, which keeps you motivated. Avalanche pays the highest interest rate first — it saves the most money. This tracker runs both on your real numbers so you can decide with confidence.

In the built-in example, paying only the minimums takes 129 months and $16,793 in interest. The avalanche method finishes in 41 months and saves $9,984.
