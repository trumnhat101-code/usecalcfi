Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

UseCalcFi ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website usecalcfi.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using UseCalcFi, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a) Calculator Inputs

All calculations on UseCalcFi run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We do not collect, transmit, or store any numbers you enter into our calculators. Your financial data never leaves your device. When you close or navigate away from the page, your inputs are gone.

b) Automatically Collected Data

When you visit UseCalcFi, our hosting provider (Cloudflare) and analytics services may automatically collect:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring URL (what site you came from)
  • Date and time of your visit

This data is used in aggregate to understand site usage and improve our calculators. We do not use it to identify individual users.

c) Contact Information

If you contact us by email, we may retain your email address and the content of your message to respond to your inquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

UseCalcFi uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the site to function. These include Cloudflare security cookies that protect against attacks.
  • Analytics cookies: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects anonymized usage data. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Advertising cookies: If Google AdSense ads are displayed on the site, Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site and other sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google's Ad Settings.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not affect calculator functionality, as all calculations are client-side and require no cookies.

3. Google AdSense and Advertising

UseCalcFi may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

4. Affiliate Links and Third-Party Services

UseCalcFi contains affiliate links to third-party financial products and services. When you click an affiliate link, you are directed to a third-party website. We may receive compensation if you complete a qualifying action (such as applying for a loan or opening an account). This compensation does not affect the accuracy of our calculators or the content of our guides.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any third-party site you visit through our links.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and improve the Site and our calculators
  • Analyze usage patterns to determine which calculators are most useful
  • Respond to your inquiries or feedback
  • Ensure the security of the Site
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention

Since we do not store calculator inputs, there is no calculator data to retain or delete. Automatically collected analytics data is retained in accordance with Google Analytics' data retention policies (default: 26 months). Contact inquiries are retained for a reasonable period to resolve the inquiry and are then deleted.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • California residents (CCPA): Right to know what personal data is collected, right to deletion, and right to opt out of sale of personal data. We do not sell personal data.
  • EU/EEA residents (GDPR): Rights of access, correction, deletion, restriction, and data portability. To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@usecalcfi.com.

8. Children's Privacy

UseCalcFi is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Security

UseCalcFi is hosted on Cloudflare Pages and served over HTTPS. Since calculator inputs never leave your device, there is no database of financial information to protect. We use industry-standard security headers (HSTS, CSP, X-Frame-Options) to protect the site from common web vulnerabilities.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
