How our calculators work

Every UseCalcFi calculator uses the same formulas banks, lenders, and the IRS use — not rough approximations. Mortgage and loan tools apply standard amortization math; our paycheck and tax calculators use current federal tax brackets, Social Security and Medicare rates, and standard deductions; retirement tools use compound growth and the 4% withdrawal rule.

We source our numbers from official, primary references — the IRS, the Social Security Administration, the Federal Reserve, and the CFPB — and update them as tax laws, contribution limits, and benchmark rates change each year. Results are estimates to help you plan; they are not financial advice, and you should confirm specifics with your lender, tax professional, or financial advisor.
