Our Mission

Financial decisions — buying a home, planning for retirement, paying off debt — are among the most consequential choices most Americans will make. Yet the tools to model these decisions have historically been locked behind expensive advisors or complicated spreadsheets.

UseCalcFi exists to change that. Every calculator on this site is free, runs entirely in your browser (your data never leaves your device), and is built with accuracy against official government sources as the top priority.

How Our Calculators Work

All UseCalcFi calculators are built with client-side JavaScript — meaning every calculation runs directly in your browser. We do not store your inputs, send your data to a server, or require an account. When you close the tab, your data is gone.

Our calculations are based on official formulas and rates from:

IRS SSA.gov Federal Reserve Fannie Mae CFPB U.S. Dept. of Education HUD / FHA U.S. Dept. of Labor

Each calculator page shows a "Last reviewed" date and source citation. We update calculations whenever tax laws, contribution limits, or official rates change — typically annually in Q1 following IRS and SSA announcements.

Editorial Standards

UseCalcFi maintains a clear separation between editorial content and commercial relationships. Our calculators and guides are written and reviewed to be accurate and unbiased. Any affiliate relationships we have with financial product providers do not influence which calculators we build or how our calculations work.

When we reference specific financial products or services in our guides, we disclose any affiliate relationship in accordance with FTC guidelines. See our full disclaimer for details.

What UseCalcFi Is Not

UseCalcFi does not provide personalized financial advice. Our calculators provide estimates based on the numbers you enter. They are educational tools — not a substitute for a licensed financial advisor, CPA, or attorney. Results may differ from your actual tax liability, loan terms, or benefit amounts. Always verify important financial decisions with a qualified professional.
